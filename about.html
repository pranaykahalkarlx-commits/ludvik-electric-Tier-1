Our Story

Who We Are

Ludvik Electric has been a trusted name in electrical services for the Denver metropolitan area, particularly within the Montbello neighborhood, since 2005. As a locally owned and operated business, we understand the unique electrical needs of homes and businesses in our community. Our team of certified and highly experienced electricians is dedicated to providing top-tier, reliable, and safe electrical solutions, from routine maintenance and complex repairs to full-scale installations and system upgrades. We pride ourselves on our meticulous attention to detail, adherence to the highest safety standards, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction. At Ludvik Electric, we believe in transparent communication, fair pricing, and delivering efficient results that stand the test of time, ensuring your property is powered safely and efficiently for years to come. Choose Ludvik Electric for unparalleled expertise and peace of mind.
